Album entries are for collections of songs in a form  of physical or digital albums.

These entries include album-related metadata (as *fields*) such as names, catalog numbers, crossfade links and *connections* to other entry types such as artists, songs and events.

A full list of album-related rules is available [here](/rules/albums)
