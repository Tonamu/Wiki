This page lists all the partner sites, VocaDB connected with over the years.

See other Vocaloid related websites [here](/docs/useful-related-informational-sites).

### Interconnected websites:

* [UtaiteDB](https://utaitedb.net/)
    * A database for Utaite music content.
    * [UtaiteDB Wiki](/docs/utaitedb)
* [TouhouDB](https://touhoudb.com/)
    * A database for Touhou music content.
    * [TouhouDB Wiki](/docs/touhoudb)

### Partner websites:

* [Project DIVA Wiki](https://projectdiva.wiki/)
    * A wiki about all games of the Project DIVA series by SEGA.
* [VOCABERLIN](https://vocaberlin.de/)
    * A vocal synth fan club stationed in Berlin, they also host related events.
* [VocaVerse Network (VVN)](https://vocaverse.network/)
    * A forum for all vocal synth fans.
* [VocalNexus & Japanradio](https://japanradio.de/)
    * A German Vocaloid fan-project and radio broadcast.
* [mikupa](https://mikupa.ru/)
    * A Russian, Vocaloid related news website and radio broadcast.
* [r/Vocaloid](https://www.reddit.com/r/Vocaloid/)
    * A subreddit for discussions and link-sharing related to Vocaloid.
