Welcome to the [VocaDB](http://vocadb.net/) wiki!

VocaDB is a collaborative database for [Vocaloid](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vocaloid), [UTAU](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Utau), and other [singing synthesizers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Speech_synthesis), with artists, discography, PVs and more. Our goal is to be the most accurate and complete source of Vocaloid discography and artists.

For more information about VocaDB, see [the complete description](/docs/footer/vocadb-what-is-it-for) and [history](/docs/documentation/vocadbs-history).

This wiki is primarily used for documenting guidelines and rules for the site.
