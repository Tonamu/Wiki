[Demo](https://vocadb.net/T/9896) songs and work in progress (WIP) songs/trailers should not be added, unless:
- The song is part of an album entry 
- The song is a [voicebank demo](https://vocadb.net/T/89)

Teasers that are different from the actual songs that they promote are allowed ([example](https://vocadb.net/S/588175)).
